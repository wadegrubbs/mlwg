Jim Is A Very Fine Cat

Jim indulges in many activities including

  • Occupying the couch
  • Watching football
  • Judging cat food varieties
  • Listening to me ponder the inequites of life

Jim will voice his concern:

  1. When our other cat Ambrose enters the room
  2. When being accidentally sat on
  3. When his litter box has not been primped and freshened

Jim is a little old man with three legs and failing kidneys, so he's probably not long for this mortal coil. Too bad. He is finest company and all cats should take a note from his friendly and pleasant demeanor.
